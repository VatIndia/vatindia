All about us

At VAT , we don’t just build aerial solutions — we redefine what’s possible in the skies.

Founded with a bold vision and backed by over 15 years of hands-on experience,
VATis a proudly 100% Indian company.
which promotes the MAKE IN INDIA initiative From blueprint to takeoff, every component, every innovation, every idea is born from our in-house capabilities.
We do not rely on foreign firms or external suppliers — a rarity in today’s aviation landscape — giving us unmatched control over quality, cost, and customization.

Our journey started with a simple but ambitious goal: to create robust, intelligent aerial systems that meet real-world demands.
Today, our fully customizable RFAs (Remotely Flying Aircraft) are a testament to that vision.
Whether navigating harsh terrains or braving extreme weather, our drones are designed to endure, adapt, and deliver with robust hardware and all-terrain, all-weather resilience.

What truly sets us apart is our skilled, passionate team, who go beyond building machines — they bring your ideas to life.
Every project is approached as a collaborative mission, where your vision is shaped by engineering precision and innovation.
The result? Less product damage, better security integration, and a system designed exactly for your needs.

At the heart of VATlies a commitment to continuous R&D, ensuring that we’re not just keeping up with technology — we’re setting the pace.
Our products are versatile, with diverse operational ranges that cater to industries from defense and agriculture to logistics and surveillance.

When you choose VAT Aviation, you're not just investing in a product — you're choosing independence, integrity, and innovation rooted in Indian excellence.
